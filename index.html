Celien Deschacht 


I study sculpture in Sint-Lucas in Ghent/ Belgium, last semester I went to Helsinki/Finland for an exchange period for four months. I actually worked mainly in the printmaking department the whole time. There I learned a lot, especially about woodcuts. 

Now back in Ghent I'm working further with the woodcut prints I made in Helsinki. And these where again developed from an previous sculpture I made in 2014-15.
For this moment my previous works are my biggest source of inspiration. 
One sculpture or prints leads me to another work of art. I see my works as one big project, a research in form, theme and materiality. 

My other Inspiration sources can be everything, from architecture to random things I see in the Dailey life. And of course the work of other artists.
